About DMMPL

Decofurn Multisolution Management Pvt Ltd (DMMPL) is a Nagpur-based ICT infrastructure pioneer, established in 2019 to deliver state-of-the-art solutions for India's evolving urban landscape.

Pioneering Urban Infrastructure Since 2019

Headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra, DMMPL was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between traditional urban services and the digital future. We specialize in the high-stakes ICT infrastructure that powers modern smart cities and safe urban environments.

Our portfolio, including major contracts with municipal corporations and Maha Metro, reflects our capacity to manage large-scale CAPEX and OPEX projects. With a robust order book of ₹350 Crores, we are committed to building the digital backbone of India's cities.
